Dr. Pournima Inamdar

Head of School of Law - MIT-WPU

About

I completed my Bachelor of Laws (LL.B) in 1998, Master of Laws (LL.M) in 2004 and my Ph.D. in 2017 from Nagpur University. I am the Head of School at Maharashtra Institute of Technology - World Peace University (MIT-WPU) Faculty of Law. Prior to joining academia, I worked as an advocate for 4 years before the Bombay High Court (Nagpur Bench). I have a total of 15 years of teaching experience in various law and management colleges across Maharashtra. Some of the prestigous institutes that I have worked at are:

  • Symbiosis Law School, Pune
  • ILS Law College
  • Bharati Vidyapeeth New Law College, Pune
  • MIT-WPU Faculty of Management

I have attended many prestigous seminars and conferences. I have also presented papers and published articles extensively in many national and international journals. The following are some of my recent publications:

  • Article published on the topic “Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar: Impact on Indian Constitution” (INDIAN JOURNAL OF APPLIED RESEARCH, Volume : 6 | Issue : 5 | May 2016 | ISSN - 2249-555X)
  • Article published on the topic “Intellectual Property Vis-à-vis Economic Development” (Business Explorer Research Journal of Business Management, ISSN No. 2319-1961, January 2016 Volume VI, page no. 26-28.)
  • Article published on the topic “ Intellectual Property Rights: Conceptual Analysis” in RTM Nagpur University’s Research Journal Special Edition on Intellectual Property Rights, October 2018, ISSN-0972-6349
